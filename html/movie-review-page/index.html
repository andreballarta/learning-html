Movie Review: Rise Beyond

Rise Beyond is about a young guitarist who wants to be famous in Seattle. Ethan Carter comes to the city with only his guitar and a big dream. He plays in small clubs and works very hard to get better. The movie shows his hard work and his passion in a real way. His success feels true, not easy. It is a nice story about following your dream.

Movie Rating:⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (9.4/10)
